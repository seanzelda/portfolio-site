WRITING

As an English major at the University of Michigan, Sean wrote many nonfiction essays, poems and short stories. In 2014, Sean was nominated for the Granader Family Prize for Excellence in Upper-Level Writing in nonfiction category. He is now a content writer for a well-known real estate company.
